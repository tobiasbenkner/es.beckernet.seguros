Our Partners

Insurance companies we work with

We work with the leading insurance companies: Allianz, AXA, Mapfre, Generali, DKV and many more.

Allianz

Founded in 1890 in Berlin, Allianz is one of the world's largest insurers. The company offers comprehensive solutions from health and life to industrial and financial insurance. With presence in over 70 countries, Allianz leads through innovation and sustainability, with focused use of cutting-edge technologies.

ASISA

Founded in 1971, this Spanish health insurer is a pioneer with a cooperative structure led by doctors. ASISA has its own network of clinics and has expanded its offerings to include dental and life insurance.

AXA

Founded in 1817 in France, AXA evolved from a local insurance mutual to a global financial group. The company operates internationally with a wide range of products and focuses on digital transformation and environmental responsibility.

Divina Seguros

Based in Valencia since 1957, this company focuses on family-oriented insurance solutions. Divina offers flexible products with a focus on personalized service without sacrificing technological modernity.

DKV

Founded in 1928 in Germany, DKV expanded to Spain in 1998. The focus is on healthcare, sustainability and digital orientation with a diversified product offering.

Euroins

Bulgaria's founding (1998) led to rapid growth in Central and Eastern Europe. Euroins focuses on various types of insurance with adaptability to emerging markets.

FIATC

Founded in Barcelona (1930) as a transport insurance cooperative, it has become an industry leader in Spain. FIATC combines traditional values with technological innovation.

Generali

Trieste's founding (1831) made Generali one of the oldest insurers in the world. The company operates in over 50 countries with a focus on sustainability and digital innovation.

Hiscox

Founded in 1901 as a British specialist insurer for marine and transport risks, Hiscox has become a global provider of highly specialized solutions (cybersecurity, art objects, SMEs).

Life 5

Young Spanish life insurance specialist with digital focus. Life 5 offers simplified, transparent products for modern customer needs.

Mapfre

Founded in 1933 as an agricultural cooperative, Mapfre grew into a global giant. With presence in 40+ countries, the company combines innovation with a solid financial position.

Mutua de Propietarios

Spain's oldest property insurer (Barcelona 1835), specialized in buildings and homeowners' associations. Modern digital services complement two centuries of experience.

HNA

Founded in 1944 for architects' social security, HNA later expanded to various sectors. The offering includes health, life, pension and savings plans.

Grupo Catalana Occidente

Founded in 1864, the group grew through acquisitions to become a global player in 50+ countries. Focus is on digitalization, sustainability and customer-oriented innovation.

Santa Lucía

Founded in 1922 specialized in funeral insurance, Santa Lucía later diversified its portfolio. With 100+ years of experience, the company combines reliability with technological progress.
